# Day 004 Real-World Examples

## Example 1: E-commerce Platform

### Business Situation

An e-commerce platform sells products online and tracks customers, orders, payments, deliveries, and reviews.

### What Goes Into the Operational Database

- Customer login details
- Product details
- Order transactions
- Payment status
- Delivery status

### What Goes Into the Data Lake

- Raw order exports
- Payment gateway JSON responses
- Website clickstream logs
- Product images
- Customer review files

### What Goes Into the Data Warehouse

- Daily sales summary
- Product performance table
- Customer purchase history
- Country-wise revenue table

### Final Dashboard or Report Use

A sales dashboard shows revenue, orders, top products, failed payments, and delivery performance.

### Business Decision Supported

The company can increase stock for popular products, improve delivery planning, and investigate payment issues.

- Account details

- Login activity

### What Goes Into the Data Lake

- Raw transaction files
- ATM logs
- Payment system responses

- PDF statements

### What Goes Into the Data Warehouse

- Branch-wise account report

- Clickstream logs
- Event tracking files

- Video ad files

### What Goes Into the Data Warehouse
