# Day 001 Real-World Examples

## Example 1: E-commerce Order Reporting

### Business Situation

An online shopping company sells products through its website and mobile app. Every day, customers place orders, make payments, cancel items, and request returns.

### What Data Is Generated

- Customer details
- Product details
- Order amount
- Payment status
- Delivery address
- Order date and time
- Return or cancellation status

### What Problem Exists

The business team wants to know daily sales, top-selling products, failed payments, and delivery performance.

But the data may be spread across many systems, such as the website database, payment system, delivery system, and customer support system.

### What a Data Engineer Does

A data engineer builds a pipeline that collects order data from different systems, cleans it, stores it in one place, and makes it ready for reporting.

The data engineer may also check for duplicate orders, missing payment details, and incorrect date formats.

### Final Business Use

The company can create dashboards that show:

- Daily revenue
- Number of orders
- Best-selling products
- Failed payments
- Delayed deliveries

This helps the business make better decisions about stock, pricing, delivery, and customer service.

## Example 2: Bank Transaction Monitoring

### Business Situation

A bank handles many transactions every day. Customers use debit cards, credit cards, online banking, mobile banking, and ATMs.

### What Data Is Generated

- Transaction amount
- Account number
- Transaction time
- Merchant name
- Location
- Payment method
- Transaction status

### What Problem Exists

The bank needs accurate transaction data for reports, customer support, and monitoring unusual activity.

If data is delayed or incorrect, the bank may miss important issues or show wrong balances in reports.

### What a Data Engineer Does

A data engineer creates systems that collect transaction data from multiple banking channels and move it into secure storage.

The engineer helps standardize formats, remove duplicate records, and make sure important transaction fields are present.

### Final Business Use

The bank can monitor transaction trends, prepare reports, support customers faster, and identify unusual transaction patterns for further review.

## Example 3: Marketing Campaign Performance Tracking

### Business Situation

A company runs ads on websites, search engines, email, and social media. The marketing team wants to understand which campaigns are working.

### What Data Is Generated

- Ad clicks
- Ad impressions
- Campaign cost
- Website visits
- Leads created
- Purchases after ad clicks
- Customer sign-ups

### What Problem Exists

Marketing data often comes from many platforms. Each platform may use different names, formats, and reporting methods.

For example, one platform may call a user action a "click", while another may call it an "engagement".

### What a Data Engineer Does

A data engineer collects marketing data from different sources, organizes it, and creates a clean dataset for analysis.

The engineer may match campaign names, standardize dates, and combine advertising cost with sales data.

### Final Business Use

The marketing team can see which campaigns bring customers, which ads waste money, and where to spend the budget next.

## Example 4: Streaming Watch History Analysis

### Business Situation

A streaming platform wants to understand what users watch, when they watch, and which shows are popular.

### What Data Is Generated

- User watch history
- Video start and stop time
- Device type
- Search history
- Likes or ratings
- Subscription status
- Region or country

### What Problem Exists

The platform may receive huge amounts of data every minute. Watch history data must be organized before it can be used for reports or recommendations.

If the data is messy, the company may misunderstand user behavior.

### What a Data Engineer Does

A data engineer builds pipelines to collect watch activity, store it, clean it, and prepare it for teams that analyze content performance.

The engineer may remove incomplete records, handle missing device information, and organize data by user, show, time, and region.

### Final Business Use

The company can understand popular shows, improve recommendations, plan new content, and measure user engagement.

## Example 5: Food Delivery App Order Tracking

### Business Situation

A food delivery app connects customers, restaurants, and delivery partners. Every order creates data from multiple steps.

### What Data Is Generated

- Customer order details
- Restaurant acceptance time
- Food preparation time
- Delivery partner assignment
- Pickup time
- Delivery time
- Payment status
- Customer rating

### What Problem Exists

The company wants to know why some orders are late, which restaurants are fast, and which areas have high demand.

This data may come from the app, restaurant system, payment system, and delivery tracking system.

### What a Data Engineer Does

A data engineer brings this data together and prepares it for reports.

The engineer may clean missing delivery times, standardize city names, and create useful tables for order tracking.

### Final Business Use

The company can improve delivery speed, support customers better, identify busy areas, and help restaurants manage demand.
